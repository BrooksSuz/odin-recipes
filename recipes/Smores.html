S'mores

Description:

S'mores are a personal favorite of mine. They are a simple dessert containing toasted marshmellow with small bars of chocolate sandwiched between a pair of graham crackers.

They're simple to make and incredibly delicious. They're most commonly enjoyed while camping!

Ingredients:

  • 1x Marshmellow
  • 1x Chocolate Bar
  • 1x Graham Cracker

Steps:

  1. Grab a graham cracker and break it into two halves.
  2. Break off 1-3 squares of chocolate from a chocolate bar.
  3. Heat up marshmellow until golden brown and add it to your graham crackers. Any common heating source will do just fine if you aren't sitting next to a campfire.
  4. Top your S'more off with the other half of the graham cracker and give light pressure to the sandwich. This will spread the marshmellow evenly and melt the chocolate.
