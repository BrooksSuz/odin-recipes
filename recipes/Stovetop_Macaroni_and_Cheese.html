Stovetop Macaroni and Cheese

Description:

This ooey-gooey recipe will have your mouth watering! A simple dish that is normally served as a side dish to a main course. However, this meal can be spiced up to your liking and served as a main dish if you'd like!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Dried Elbow Macaroni Noodles
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 6 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 3/4 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot with 4 quarts of water to a boil. Mix in 2 tsp salt and your pasta. Reference the instructions for cooking the pasta on the packaging provided.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, begin to heat your cream in a heavy saucepan over medium heat. The cream will come to a light bubble. Combine and mix in the cheeses as well as 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper. Stir until the cheese is noticeably melted and lightly bubbling. Continue to stir for 5-7 minutes.
  3. As soon as the noodles are cooked, drain your pasta and add them to the cheese sauce. Stir until combined.
  4. Macaroni and cheese is very cutomizable. Add anything else that you think may enhance the flavor and dig in!
